Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction thriller film directed by Christopher Nolan. The film follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who steals information by infiltrating the subconscious of his targets. He is given a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for the implantation of another person's idea into a target's subconscious.
